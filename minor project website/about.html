About Us



Hermes Voyage was founded by Balamurali Krishna S and Neksha Gupta in 2022.


At first glance, it could appear to be a typical travel website, but there is a significant distinction; our primary goal is to give consumers the trip of a lifetime. If they so desire, everything will be in their hands. Not only do we allow the consumer to pick the location, but the entire package may be tailored to their needs, preferences, and/or budget, which is typically not offered by other travel agencies since they only provide a limited number of pre-built packages and cannot be tailored to the customer's desires.
